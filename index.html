What is Formula 1

Formula 1 is a auto racing event that occurs internationally between 10 teams of 2 drivers. Formula 1 has two different championships, the drivers championship and constructors championship. The drivers championship is an individual standing, that adds up the scores from each Grand Prix race. The constructors championship is the overall standing of the team. The drivers championship is important because the winner is recognized as the best driver of the season and he will gain all the fame and prestige that comes with winning the drivers championship, and winning the constructors championship means the team that won will recieve the highest budget for the upcoming season.
